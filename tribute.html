ROHIT SHARMA

THE HITMAN

  • Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer who currently captains the India national cricket team across all formats.
  • He is a right-handed batsman. Considered one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of the greatest opening batters of all time, Sharma is known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities and leadership skills.
  • He plays for Mumbai Indians in IPL and for Mumbai in domestic cricket.
  • Double Centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs): Rohit Sharma holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs. He scored three double centuries - 209 against Australia in 2013, 264 against Sri Lanka in 2014, and 208* against Sri Lanka in 2017.
  • Five Centuries in a Single World Cup: In the 2019 ICC Cricket World Cup, Rohit Sharma became the first player to score five centuries in a single edition of the tournament.
  • Highest Individual Score in T20 Internationals: Sharma scored an impressive 118 runs off 43 balls against Sri Lanka in a T20 International in 2017, which was the highest individual score in T20Is at that time.
  • Fastest T20I Century: Rohit Sharma shared the record for the fastest century in T20 Internationals, reaching the milestone in just 35 balls against Sri Lanka in 2017.
  • Indian Premier League (IPL) Success: As the captain of the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League, Rohit Sharma led the team to multiple championships, establishing himself as one of the most successful captains in IPL history.
  • Test Captaincy: Rohit Sharma has also been appointed as the captain of the Indian Test cricket team, taking over the role during India's tour of South Africa in December 2021.
  • Arjuna Award: In 2015, Rohit Sharma was honored with the Arjuna Award, one of India's prestigious awards recognizing outstanding achievements in sports.
